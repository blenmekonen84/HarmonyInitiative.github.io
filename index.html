Harmony-Initiative 

Our Mission

Welcome to Harmony Initiative, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students of color through educational support, mentorships, and community engagement. Our mission is to foster academic excellence and personal growth among Black students, creating a supportive network that helps them thrive both academically and personally. We understand that Black students face unique challenges and obstacles in their academic journey, and our goal is to provide them with the tools, resources, and support they need to overcome these challenges and achieve their full potential.

History

Established in 2017, while Harmony Initiative was first founded to empower Black students it has now refocused on a bigger stance and strives to help all students of color. Since 2017 Harmony Initiative has been actively working to bridge educational gaps, promote inclusivity, and empower students of color to achieve their full potential.

Our Upcoming Gala 

"Elevate, Educate, Empower: A Night For Maryland's Black Students"

Join us for an extraordinary evening as we gather to celebrate and support the educational journey of Maryland's Black students. The "Elevate, Educate, Empower" gala is a pivotal event organized by the Harmony Initiative to raise funds for scholarship money to award black student of Maryland and raise awareness to our mission of empowering excellence within the Black student community.

Our work

As a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering Black students, Harmony Initiative is committed to creating a supportive network for academic excellence and personal growth. We believe that every student, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential. Through educational support, mentorships, and community engagement, we aim to provide Black students with the tools and resources they need to succeed.
